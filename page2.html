Why I Love my Family!

My family is everything to me! They support and respect my personal choices.

My mom somehow knows exactly where everything is, even when I’m sure it’s lost forever. My dad works as a cook and I love his cooking more than my moms', though I would never admit it. My parents help me with paying the remaining school fees after my scholarship. My older sister teaches me almost everything from how to dress formally to how taxes work. Being part of a family is wonderful!
